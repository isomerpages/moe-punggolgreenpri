**<font size=6>String Ensemble</font>**

PGPS String Ensemble was formed in 2015. The CCA provides our young musicians with an opportunity to work together as a team to showcase their musical talents and form lasting friendships. We prepare our members for performances for Open House, Aesthetics Week and other events.

  

Not only do we look for opportunities to perform, we also look for opportunities to learn from others. We are always looking out for talented students who are able to play well. We welcome students with violin, viola, cello and double bass or relevant music background to sign up.

  

Recruitment is conducted twice a year and an audition will be conducted before confirmation of membership.
