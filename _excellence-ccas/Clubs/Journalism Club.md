**<font size=6>Journalism Club</font>**

The PGPS Journalism Club aims to foster studentsâ€™ interest in journalism. Over time, students will acquire a whole spectrum of journalism skills, such as planning articles, interviewing & reporting, newswriting, photography as well as newspaper layout and design.

  

We provide our students with skills to be able to have the capability, courage, and confidence to gather information from different sources and express their views convincingly and comprehensively.

  

It is our considered view that the activities we conduct are a great extension of our reading, writing and thinking skills. We endeavour to create a student newsletter in the long run; in the immediate future, our main goal is for our students to experience learning in fun and creative ways.
