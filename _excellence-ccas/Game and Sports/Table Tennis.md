**<font size=6>Table Tennis</font>**

Table Tennis develops the students in terms of their responsiveness, reflexes and reactions as well as their hand-eye coordination. In addition to sporting excellence, we also aim to instil in our students the values of good sportsmanship and work ethic and to cultivate a love for the sport.

  

Students can participate in our CCA from Primary 3 onwards where they will learn tactics and techniques specific to Table Tennis.
