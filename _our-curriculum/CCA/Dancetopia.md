**<font size=6>Dancetopia</font>**

“Dancetopia”, our resident PGPS Dance Club, was formally created in 2015. Their performances comprise primarily Contemporary Dance, which remains the cornerstone of their identity as a Dance Institution. As part of their training, there is a focus on character-building, cultivating the right values and possessing a good foundation of dance technique.

  

Under the tutelage of both our teachers and instructors, the Club has grown exponentially over the past few years to its current size of more than 50 dancers. The club aims to participate in both external performances and competitions to not only gain more exposure and experience, but to also fly the PGPS flag while displaying their wide array of talents!
