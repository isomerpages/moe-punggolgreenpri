**<font size=6>Softball</font>**

Softball is a game similar to baseball. It is a great team game played on the field that comprises catching, running and throwing (pitching) as well as batting. A game which espouses fun - while also inculcating both a competitive spirit and values of sportsmanship and fair play at the same time - and can also be played at the primary school level.

  

In line with our School Values, the softball CCA in Punggol Green Primary School provides students with a platform to experience and practice other values such as cohesion, dedication, respect, work ethic, a desire to excel, support for one another while also having fun.

  

Currently, we are in the process of finalising the first-ever batch of Softball students for Punggol Green Primary School. The new team will work towards the goal of participating in Softball competitions in the near future.
