**<font size=6>Basketball</font>**

Basketball is co-conducted by the teachers and an experienced coach. We aim to bring out the best in out students and help them achieve excellence in and out of the court.

  

During our training on Mondays, our students are coached to improve in different areas such as offensive and defensive game play, shooting skills etc. Drills are also carried out regularly to develop the students' stamina, physical abilities and mental strength to cope with the demands and challenges of the game.
