**<font size=5>The Caring Teacher Awards (CTA)</font>**

The Caring Teacher Awards (CTA) is a biennial event that pays tribute to teachers who show care and concern or the holistic development of their students.  It recognises the efforts of those who go the extra mile to ensure their charges grow up to be confident and independent learners in school and outside classroom.

  
Congratulations to the following teachers who have been nominated for the CTA 2022. Thank you to our students and parents who have nominated our teachers.
